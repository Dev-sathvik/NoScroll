What's This?

A Tool for improving your productivity by restricting consumption of short video formates like reels.
Step 1

Enroll

Navigate to "Enroll" to be a part of the community of noscrollers!.

Step 2

Provide your motive.

Enter your strong reason that leads you to stop scrolling.

Step 3

Watch your people

See all the people like you who have enrolled.

I have always wanted to stop my doomscrolling habit, but it was quite hard. It would reduce my productivity and affect my daily chores. This is why i imagined a platform where one could meet similar kind of people experiencing the same issue as mine. This would motivate me to stop scrolling knowing that there are people just like me.

It works by giving you a option to enroll yourself to noscroll platform and see other like minded people that have enrolled already. You would be able to see a strong community of noscrollers who have promised themselves not to scroll anymore.

  • Watch other enrolled people like you.
  • See the statistics of the noscroll platform
  • Track your time saving and streak
